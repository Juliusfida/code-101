how to make kdf

  1. Sieve the plain flour and salt and then add baking powder. Set aside
  2. In a large bowl, use a wooden spoon to beat together margarine and sugar until creamy.
  3. Mix into this one egg at a time followed by milk. Add the flour mixture into this and knead into a dough.
  4. Let rest for 30 minutes.
  5. Use a pizza cutter or knife to cut out small portions of the dough in small rectangles.
  6. Fry the dough in hot oil for 6-8 minutes or until golden brown.
